About ZapNest

We believe great tech shouldn't require endless research.

Our Mission

ZapNest was born from a simple frustration: finding quality tech accessories takes too much time. Reviews are paid, listings are cluttered, and most products underwhelm.

We test 50+ products every month across categories—audio, charging, organisation, connectivity. Only one makes the cut. That's what ships in your ZapNest Black Box.

The Team

We're a small team of tech enthusiasts and product designers based in India. We obsess over build quality, materials, and whether something actually improves daily life. No affiliates, no sponsored picks—just honest curation.

Why Subscription?

Discovery is part of the joy. Each month brings a new category, a new surprise. The subscription model lets us negotiate better pricing, test at scale, and pass savings to you.

Quick Questions

Who runs ZapNest?

A small team of product enthusiasts in India who test tech for a living.

No. We purchase products independently and have no affiliate relationships.
