Shipping & Delivery

Free delivery across India on all ZapNest subscriptions.

Delivery Timeline

All ZapNest boxes ship between the 15th and 20th of each month. Delivery typically takes 3-7 business days depending on your location. Metro cities usually receive within 3-4 days.

Tracking

Once your box ships, you'll receive a tracking link via email and WhatsApp. Track in real-time through our logistics partner's portal.

Shipping Costs

Shipping is free on all tiers—Lite, Core, and Elite. No hidden charges, no minimum order value. The price you see is the price you pay.

Delivery Partners

We work with trusted logistics partners including Delhivery and BlueDart to ensure safe and timely delivery. All packages are insured during transit.

Shipping FAQ

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we only ship within India. International shipping is planned for 2026.

What if my package is damaged?

Contact us within 48 hours with photos. We'll send a replacement at no cost.
